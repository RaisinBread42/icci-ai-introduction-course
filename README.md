# AI for Real Life — ICCI

A practical, no-code introduction to AI taught at the **International College of the Cayman Islands (ICCI)**.

Five weeks. Two hours a week. No prerequisites.

Students train their own AI, look inside how it works, catch it lying, and audit its biases — walking out with practical skills, real artifacts, and a clear-eyed understanding of the tools.

This repository hosts the course landing page (`index.html`).
